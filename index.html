Hi, i'm Wajdy.
A Frontend Web Developer.

I'm currently focused on frontend development, but I hope to expand my skills into Full Stack Development by May 2024. My expertise revolves around crafting engaging web experiences using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and more.

About Me


I'm a second-year Computer Engineering student at the University of Ottawa, deeply passionate about front-end development. My journey into web development began during my teenage years when I initially used Shopify's page builders to create websites. However, my desire to take control and develop sites from scratch led me to pursue a path as a full-stack developer.

Over time, I honed my web design and development skills, creating online stores for personal projects and clients. This journey eventually led me to freelance work on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and Fiverr, where I've earned a five-star reputation as a website designer.

Fun fact about me: I'm a moderator of a Shopify help group for beginners with 60k+ members. Everyday I dedicate some time to lend a helping hand to those in need of it free of charge.

Shopify Website Design Projects

Introducing MyShoeCharms. After giving Hussam a public review of his shop (before the re-design), he reached out to me thanking me. Few weeks later, he reaches out asking for a website re-design so I assisted him. He was transferring from Etsy to Shopify so he found himself struggling to navigate through Shopify. We encountered quite a few bugs in the theme and unfortunately support was of no help so I messed around with the code and eventually got everything working the way it should be. I was very thankful for his generous tip because he saw the amount of time I was putting into debugging the theme.

Introducing YourVinylSource. Once of the most succesful stores i've built powered by a lovely lady named Renatta. This store is also the largest tip i've ever received ($200). I met Renatta through the FB group. We hopped on a call, spoke about her requirements and immediately got started on her store design. To this day she continuously recommends me to her friends and reaches out for minor additions.

Introducing IronThreads, one of my favourite projects. Iron Threads is a POD clothing store. In my shopify for beginners facebook group, I saw Jason leaving a post asking for web designing services and I left him a comment letting him know he can reach out to me and he did just that. We spoke for a few days and eventually got started. This was one of my longer projects but I was very happy with the end result and so was he. To this day, he recommends everyone in the FB group to reach out to me should they need web design services.

Introducing Kitchen outlet. A dropshipping store full of kitchen appliances. I reached out to Muntakim (the store owner) through my instagram business page. He was very skeptical but also very curious about dropshipping. He has never done something like this before but after speaking with him for several days, he decided he wanted to work with me so i redirected him to my Fiverr profile and began the order. He was extremely satisfied with store.

Note: this store is no longer live.

Introducing scrubOmatic. scrubOmatic is a 3 product branded dropshipping store that sells cleaning tools. This store was one of my longer projects as I ended up doing a lot more than what was originally agreed upon. I was only supposed to create 1 custom made page but because I want to keep my clients satisfied I ended doing 3 custom made pages. Unfortunately, she had to shut down her store as she is a single mom of 3 kids and couldn't keep up with everything but working with her was a pleasure nontheless.

Note: this store is no longer live.

The store owner wanted a clothing store that seperates men and womens clothing in an organized fashion and i did just that by using custom made sections.
